# Marketing UI kit — Spoonflower Seller Lab Pro

The public-facing site for Seller Lab Pro.

## Files

- `index.html` — full marketing page assembled from components.
- `components.jsx` — Nav, Hero, FeatureGrid, ProductShot, PricingTable, Testimonial, FAQ, Footer.

## Sections (in order)

1. **Nav** — brand lockup left, mid-page anchors center, "Open app" CTA right.
2. **Hero** — display headline with italic accent, lead, two CTAs, shimmer watermark.
3. **Featurelet row** — three short value propositions under the hero.
4. **Product shot** — visual of the dashboard with annotations pointing to features.
5. **Feature grid** — four feature cards with quatrefoil bullets.
6. **Testimonial** — single big quote from a maker.
7. **Pricing** — two plans (Free / Pro) with the Pro plan featured.
8. **FAQ** — collapsible questions.
9. **Footer** — small, two columns: links + the "made by zabzablab" line.

## Voice

Plainspoken, makers-talking-to-makers. No "unlock", no "elevate". The italic in the headline carries the warmth.
